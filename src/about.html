Granite, Marble, Limestone

We have direct access to quarries in many countries, and work with major distributors in Italy, Brazil, Spain, India and China. Delivery is available to many locations. If you know what you want but are not in the area, contact us today to inquire about shipping options.

We have many years of experience in this industry, know what we are talking about and how to appreciate a good relationship with our customers!

Did you know?

Granite is recognized as one of the most beautiful and reliable materials. It is often used for countertops in bathrooms, stands and kitchens. Granite is heat and water resistant, very tough and hard, which makes it virtually unbreakable. It keeps its form and color and does not scratch as easily as other surfaces.
